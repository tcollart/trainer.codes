Can Pokemon Go friends see your location?

No, you're safe, Pokemon GO friends cannot see your exact location. However they can estimate where you are looking at the gifts you send them.

Can you trade Pokemon with someone far away?

Yes you can trade Pokémon with someone far away but the Stardust cost will increase.

Does sending a gift increase friendship level?

Yes and No, sending a gift doesn't increase the friendship level between two trainers. Your friendship level will be increased when the trainer you sent a gift to actually opens the gift. Opening gifts grants friendship levels XP in both directions.

How can I be a lucky friend?

In order to become lucky friend with someone you'll need to be Best Friend with that trainer and, in the same day, Trade a Pokémon with your friend, Battle in Raid Battles with your best friend, and Opening a gift from your friend.

How many Pokemon friends can you have?

You can have up to 200 friends in Pokemon GO! Remember to remove old friends who stop playing and add active ones with Trainer Codes!

How many gifts can you open a day?

You can send up to 20 Gifts to your friends in Pokemon GO every day. You can also hold up to 10 gifts in your inventory. Sending Gifts gives 200XP and increases friendship levels.
